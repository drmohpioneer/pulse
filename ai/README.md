# AI

Purpose:

Hold AI-related prompts, evaluation fixtures, model integration notes, and future extraction experiments.

Pulse AI must reason over structured Clinical State, not raw audio or unbounded transcript context.

Rules:

- LLMs do not own clinical state.
- LLMs do not maintain timers.
- LLMs do not invent clinical events.
- Low-confidence evidence requires confirmation.

- extraction experiments
